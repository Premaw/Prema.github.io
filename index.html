COVID-19 Cases vs Deaths

This project demonstrates the number of cases, deaths, and vaccinations during the COVID-19 pandemic. It includes data from around the world from late January 2020 to early August 2022. With respect to people who lost their loved ones during the pandemic, the visualisation allows us to see the variation in the numbers over 231 locations. Globally, the number of cases skyrocketed from the end of December 2021 to the middle of January 2022. However, the number of deaths during that period was significantly lower than in January 2021, when the peak of cases occurred.

  • Date cleaning and exploration: SQL and Excel
  • Visualisation: Tableau
  • Data source: https://ourworldindata.org
